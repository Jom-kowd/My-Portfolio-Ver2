My Story

I wanted to understand how it worked, what made it tick, and how I could make it do more. This curiosity was the first step on my path to becoming a developer.

Today, I am a passionate IT student focused on building intuitive and powerful applications. What started as a dream to be a pilot has transformed into a mission to navigate the endless skies of technology.
